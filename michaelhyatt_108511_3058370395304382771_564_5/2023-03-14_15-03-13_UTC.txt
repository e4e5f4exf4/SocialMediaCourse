Our inner Narrator, while usually well-intentioned, is not omniscient. It’s limited. And sometimes, those limitations leave us stranded by the wayside of life or frustrated we can’t go further, faster.⁠
⁠
If you want to get unstuck, take a good hard look at the stories you're telling yourself about your situation. Is it true? Is it helpful? Is there a better, more empowering story I could tell myself?⁠
